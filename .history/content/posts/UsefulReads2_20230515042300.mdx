# Wendy's to Test Groundbreaking AI at the Drive-Thru
<br/>
Wendy's, the popular fast-food chain, is partnering with Google Cloud to launch an exciting new initiative in AI technology. This pilot program, set to debut in June at a Columbus, Ohio restaurant, will feature Wendy's FreshAI, a cutting-edge system designed to streamline the drive-thru experience. By using AI technology, Wendy's will be able to engage in conversations with customers, take customized orders, and answer frequently asked questions, all in an effort to provide top-tier customer service. Google's impressive generative AI and large language models will be seamlessly integrated with Wendy's menu and business rules to provide the best possible experience. Joining several other fast-food chains already utilizing AI for their drive-thru operations, Wendy's and Google Cloud are sure to revolutionize the industry. With an existing partnership since 2021, Wendy's and Google Cloud are poised to change the way people experience fast food forever.

# Professionals who fear losing their jobs to artificial intelligence
<br/>
The rise of artificial intelligence (AI) has sparked apprehension among professionals across diverse domains, raising concerns about the future of their careers. With the advent of AI technology like OpenAI's ChatGPT, there is a prevailing fear that machines will gradually replace human workers, matching or even surpassing their performance. Reports from reputable sources like Goldman Sachs and PwC have underscored the potential for AI to disrupt numerous job sectors worldwide. However, industry experts emphasize a different perspective: embracing AI as a valuable resource rather than perceiving it as a threat. They urge professionals to invest in learning and understanding AI, enabling them to adapt and collaborate harmoniously with this transformative technology, thereby ensuring their continued relevance and worth in the dynamic job market.
