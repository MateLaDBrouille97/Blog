In a live broadcast titled "La guerre des intelligences ? Laurent Alexandre [EN DIRECT]," Laurent Alexandre delved into the ongoing battle between human intelligence and artificial intelligence. He emphasized the pivotal role of education in preparing for an AI-driven future and expressed concerns about AI's impact on employment and job displacement. Ethical considerations surrounding AI development and usage were thoroughly explored, along with the potential for AI to enhance human capabilities. Laurent Alexandre also highlighted the importance of public discourse and policymaking in shaping the future of AI and examined its global implications.
