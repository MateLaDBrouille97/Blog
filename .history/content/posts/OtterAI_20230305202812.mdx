# What is Otter AI ?
<br/>
Otter.ai is an AI-powered transcription service that uses natural language processing technology to convert human speech into text. 
With speaker detection, it allows people to record and transcribe voice conversations in different settings, providing a solution to the time-consuming and high cost of manual transcription. 
Otter.ai is free to use with custom pricing plans, and it includes a web, Chrome extension, and mobile version. 
It has features like custom vocabulary, speaker recognition and voiceprint, meeting recording, and the ability to export documents in different formats.
