# What is Runway ML?
<br/>
Runway ML is a versatile platform that utilizes artificial intelligence to create, transform, and combine images. With an impressive array of AI-powered tools, it enables creative professionals to edit and enhance videos, photos, and other forms of media.
<br/>
<Youtube id= "trXPfpV5iRQ"/>
<br/>
One of the most popular tools offered by Runway ML is the Remove Background feature, which allows users to remove the background of images and videos with just a few clicks. This is an incredibly useful tool for those who want to add their own background or simply remove distracting elements from their media.
<br/>
Another powerful tool is Text to Image Generation, which generates realistic images from text descriptions. This can be useful for creating images for marketing campaigns or product descriptions, and can save valuable time compared to manually creating images.
<br/>
Runway ML's Image to Image tool is also noteworthy, allowing users to transform images in various ways. For example, it can transform black and white images into color, or even create a more painterly or stylized effect.
<br/>
Other impressive features include Inpainting, Replace Background, Blur Background, Export Alpha Matte, Motion Tracking, Erase and Replace, Text to 3D Texture, Clean Audio, Generate Transcription, Text to Color Grade, Infinite Image, and Frame Interpolation. These features offer a wide range of possibilities for enhancing and customizing media content.
<br/>
Runway ML's platform is designed to be accessible to a wide range of users, from communicators and educators to engineers and artists. Its AI Magic Tool offers an intuitive user interface that simplifies the process of creating and transforming images, making it a great choice for those who want to add AI-powered capabilities to their creative workflows.
<br/>
Overall, Runway ML is a powerful and versatile platform that offers a suite of AI-powered tools for enhancing and customizing media content. Its wide range of capabilities make it a valuable asset for creative professionals in a variety of fields.
