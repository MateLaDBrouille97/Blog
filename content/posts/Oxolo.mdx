Oxolo AI is AI-powered application focused on breaking down barriers in online marketing and content creation. 
<br/>
They provide a user-friendly online video maker that uses AI to simplify the video production process for e-commerce sellers and small business owners. With just a URL, Oxolo generates high-quality marketing videos that are ready in minutes. 
<br/>
The platform supports multiple languages, offers AI actors with human emotion, and provides a library of images, videos, and music. 
<br/>
Oxolo videos can be easily edited and updated, and the platform offers 24/7 customer support. Pricing varies based on features and usage.
