# What is Gamma?
<br/>
Organizations can use Gamma AI's enterprise data provisioning platform to prevent breaches from malicious and negligent security blunders over SaaS applications. The platform offers cloud data discovery and data classification to automate the process of identifying and protecting sensitive data.

Gamma AI's cloud DLP tool continuously monitors employees and notifies administrators when an employee makes a security mistake that could lead to a data breach. The tool offers horizontal coverage with one-click deployment across all devices, applications, and users.
<br/>
Gamma AI is highly customizable to fit specific business needs and requirements and is compatible with various regulatory standards and data protection laws. The deployment time of Gamma AI varies depending on the size and complexity of the organization. Typically, it takes a few days to install and configure Gamma AI.
<br/>
One of the unique features of Gamma AI is its ability to create engaging content using AI. This feature provides businesses with the opportunity to deliver customized, engaging content to their employees, making it easier for them to understand and follow data protection policies.
